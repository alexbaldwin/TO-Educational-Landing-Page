# Summary

The life counter allows you to store the values of your characterâ€™s life or any other values. Fusion 360 source files are included.

What files to print:
Ring 3 pcs
Side 2 pcs
Center-cylinder 1 pcs

If you want a different number of rings, you can change the length of the cylinder: 5 mm * number of rings + 6 mm.

# Print Settings

Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.12 (ring, side); 0.2 (cylinder)
Infill: 20%

# Post-Printing

I recommend to glue the sides and cylinder.

If the rings rotate too easily, they can be pressed against the sides; for this, the cylinder is made 1 mm shorter. You can also print rings with brim inside (and cut later)
